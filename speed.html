Speed

  • Release date : June 10th, 1994
  • Run time : 1 hour & 56 minutes
  • Rating: R
Speed centers around Los Angeles S.W.A.T Officer Jack Travern, who foils the extortion plot of a bomber, causing the bomber to retaliate against Travern. The bomber does this by rigging a bus with explosives so that it will explode if the bus is going under 50 miles per hour. It becomes Travern's job to try and save the people on the bus from death, while stopping the bomber and his plot to extort the city.
